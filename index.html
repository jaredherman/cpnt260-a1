Where We Were Founded

Alberto Restaurant was founded and created in Calgary, Alberta by a man named Alberto Hernanez in 1964.

Alberto Hernanez

Alberto Hernanez grew up in the city of Calgary, Alberta. Since he was young he had the dream of opening his own restaurant and after several years of working for different restaurants he had a chance to open his own Alberto Restaurant.
